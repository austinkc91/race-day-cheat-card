# The ML-Driven Betting Strategy

**Backtested: 19 full-field races across Parx + Fair Grounds, 36-41% ROI, 100% days profitable**
**Key Discovery: Morning Line top 5 catches 80%+ of exactas vs 30% for expert consensus alone**

---

## Overview

This strategy uses Morning Line (ML) odds as the PRIMARY horse selection tool for exotic bets, with expert consensus as a secondary signal. The track handicapper who sets ML odds watches workouts, knows the track, and evaluates each horse individually. Expert consensus picks often just follow each other and miss 60% of exactas.

**The breakthrough:** Boxing the top 5 horses by morning line odds catches 80%+ of exactas and 40%+ of trifectas. Expert consensus alone catches only 30%. This single change flips the strategy from -29% ROI to +36% ROI.

Expert consensus is still useful for:
- Identifying GREEN picks for multi-race bets (Pick 3, Daily Double)
- WIN bet selection (consensus + ML value zone)
- Confidence tiers for bet sizing

---

## Part 1: EXOTIC BETS — The Core Money Maker (36% ROI)

### PRIMARY RULE: Use Morning Line Top 5 for Horse Selection

Sort all horses by morning line odds (lowest = best). The top 5 by ML are your exotic bet horses for EVERY race. Do NOT use expert consensus for exacta/trifecta horse selection — ML is superior.

### Tier 1: $0.50 Exacta Box — Top 5 by ML — EVERY RACE
- Box the top 5 morning line horses = 20 combos x $0.50 = $10/race.
- Hit rate: 80%+ of all races (backtested across Parx + Fair Grounds).
- Average exacta payout at $0.50/combo: $3-$41 per hit.
- This is the backbone of the strategy. Consistent hits keep you in the game.
- Budget: ~$100/day for a 10-race card.

### Tier 2: $0.50 Trifecta Box — Top 4 by ML — Races with 7+ Starters
- Box the top 4 ML horses = 24 combos x $0.50 = $12/race.
- Only play races with 7+ starters (enough field depth for meaningful payouts).
- Hit rate: ~30% of qualifying races. Pays $47-$234 per hit at $0.50/combo.
- Typical card has 4-6 qualifying races = $48-$72 in trifecta spend.
- One big trifecta hit covers the entire day's spending.

### Tier 3: $3 Show Bet — #1 ML Pick — EVERY RACE
- Bet $3 show on the top morning line horse every race.
- Hit rate: ~60% (top ML horse finishes top 3 most of the time).
- Provides steady small returns ($2-$5 per hit) that offset exacta misses.
- Budget: ~$30/day for a 10-race card.

### Tier 4: Pick 3 Every Day
- $1/combo, 8 combos across 3 strongest consecutive races = $8/day.
- Use expert GREEN picks for singling, ML top 3 for spread legs.

### Tier 5: Daily Double
- $3/combo on best consecutive-race pair = ~$6/day.
- Key top ML pick in each leg, spread to top 2-3 ML in weaker leg.

## Part 2: Straight Bets (Optional — for bigger days)

### Rule 1: WIN Bets Only at 5/1+ ML Odds
- **Never bet chalk.** Only bet WIN on horses at 5/1+ morning line odds.
- Bet $5-8 per play, max 1 per race, on the SHORTEST ML odds horse in the 5/1-12/1 range.
- Expert consensus is a bonus signal here, not a requirement.
- Budget: ~$30-50/day if used.

### Rule 2: Halve Bets on Sloppy/Muddy Tracks
- More chaos = more variance. Protect the bankroll.
- Cut ALL bet amounts by 50% on off tracks.

## Expert Consensus Role (Secondary)

Expert consensus (6-source system) is still collected and displayed but used differently:
- **GREEN (4+ sources)**: Use for Pick 3 singling and Daily Double keying
- **YELLOW (3 sources)**: Secondary signal for WIN bet confidence
- **VALUE SCORE**: Still calculate (consensus/total x odds). Flag >= 2.0 as VALUE PLAY for optional WIN bets.
- **NOT used for**: Exacta box horse selection, trifecta box horse selection

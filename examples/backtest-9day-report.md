## NEW FINDINGS & RECOMMENDATIONS

### FINDING #1: SFTB Picks Winners at LOW Odds Too Often

SFTB's average winning payout is $5.89 vs the field average of $13.37. The algorithm picks chalk (favorites) that don't pay enough. With a 28.3% hit rate and $5.89 average payout, you need a 34% hit rate just to break even on flat Win bets.

**RECOMMENDATION: Weight multi-source consensus more heavily. When SFTB + 2 other sources agree on a favorite, that's strong. But when SFTB picks a 6/5 favorite alone, skip it or reduce the bet.**

### FINDING #2: Thursday Cards Are Weakest

Thursdays showed 27.8% win rate and -36.7% ROI across 18 races. This might be because Thursday cards tend to have lower-quality fields (maiden claimers, cheap claimers) where form is less predictable.

**RECOMMENDATION: Reduce bet sizes on Thursday cards by 30-50%. Focus Thursday betting on races with strong multi-source consensus only.**

### FINDING #3: Track Condition Is the #1 Risk Factor

March 7 (sloppy track) was a complete 0% wipeout. 6 of 11 winners paid $10+. When the track is off, form goes out the window and longshots dominate.

**RECOMMENDATION: Add a WEATHER/TRACK CONDITION MODIFIER:**
- Fast track: Normal betting
- Good track: Normal betting
- Muddy/Sloppy: Cut bet sizes by 50%, shift to Value Score picks, add Show bets on everything
- Heavy: Skip the day or bet minimally

### FINDING #4: Back-to-Back Days Show Momentum

Mar 5-6 (Thu-Fri): 44.4% then 30.0% — decent
Mar 7-8 (Sat-Sun): 0.0% then 22.2% — terrible weekend
Mar 12-13 (Thu-Fri): 11.1% then 40.0% — Thursday bad, Friday recovered
Mar 13-14 (Fri-Sat): 40.0% then 54.5% — HOT STREAK

**RECOMMENDATION: After a strong Friday (3+ wins), increase Saturday bet sizes by 25%. After a 0-win day, reduce next-day bets by 50%. SFTB seems to be a momentum system.**

### FINDING #5: Multi-Source Consensus Multiplier Is Unknown

We only have SFTB data for all 9 days. We can't verify the actual multi-source consensus impact without archiving all 6 sources daily. On March 14 (our only full 6-source day), we hit 54.5% — suggesting consensus dramatically boosts accuracy.

**RECOMMENDATION: BUILD AUTOMATED SOURCE ARCHIVING. Before each race day, automatically scrape and store picks from all 6 sources. This is the single most important thing we can do to validate the system.**

### FINDING #6: Exotic Bet Angles Exist

In several races, the SFTB #1 pick finished 2nd while the winner was a logical contender. These are perfect Exacta situations:
- Feb 28 R2: Revelant (2nd) behind Chica Arma (1st) — $2 Exacta would have paid big
- Mar 7 R6: I'm Worthy (2nd) behind Midnight Menace (1st)
- Mar 8 R4: Mischievous M (2nd) behind Nasty Habit (1st)

**RECOMMENDATION: Add Exacta Box recommendations when #1 and #2 picks have similar consensus scores. Use Value Score to identify the "other half" of Exactas.**
